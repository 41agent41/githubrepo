## B2B platforms — SAP Ariba Network (GNFR)

Coles uses **SAP Ariba Network** for **Goods Not for Resale (GNFR)** — i.e. suppliers that provide goods and services for building, operating, maintaining, and digitizing stores, distribution centers, production sites, transport, and community support.

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **RFx and sourcing** | Requests for information, proposals, quotes; online auctions; contract negotiations. |
| **Purchase-to-Pay** | Ordering, fulfillment, invoicing; digitized Source-to-Pay with visibility and security. |
| **Contracts** | Electronic execution via DocuSign. |
| **Participation** | Free for suppliers. |
| **Support** | Enterprise account suppliers: Ariba Help Centre. All GNFR suppliers: **gnfrsuppliersupport@coles.com.au** (process, functional, technical). |

**Transacting with Coles:** Purchase Orders (primary for most GNFR goods/services) and Invoice Against Contract (for project-based or recurring services with pre-negotiated agreements). Ariba Discovery can be used for global opportunities.
