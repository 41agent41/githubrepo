## EDI standards and message types

ALDI Australia uses **EANCOM** (UN/EDIFACT-based) standards for business partners.

| Document type | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| **INVOIC** | Invoices (EDIFACT). |
| **ORDERS** | Purchase orders (EDIFACT). |
| **DESADV** | Despatch advice / warehouse delivery notifications (EDIFACT). |

**Logistics (ALDI AHEAD):** Logistics service providers follow ALDI AHEAD guidelines, including messages such as IFTMIN, IFTSTA, and related freight messages. Details are in the official EDI Message Integration Guides (see URLs above).

## Supplier requirements and standards

Suppliers must comply with (summary; see corporate.aldi.com.au for current versions):

- Code of Conduct and Corporate Responsibility Principles  
- ALDI Business Partner Sustainability Standards (e.g. applicable from Feb 2024)  
- ALDI Stores Supplier Standards  
- Quality assurance (e.g. Fresh Produce Surveillance Testing)  
- Sustainable packaging and forestry policies  
- **Electronic Data Interchange (EDI)** requirements  
- Chain of responsibility and logistics standards  

ALDI works with **1,000+ Australian businesses**; strong focus on Australian-made (e.g. 97% fruit and vegetables, 100% eggs, milk, fresh meat, fresh salmon, fresh poultry from Australian farms). Long-term supplier relationships; simplified processes; high-volume, timely orders. Small Business Supplier Payment Policy and quarterly Business Partner newsletters are available.
