# Value-Add Network and B2B/B2C Partners — Drakes Supermarkets (Australia)

**Retailer:** Drakes Supermarkets  
**Web:** [www.drakes.com.au](https://www.drakes.com.au)

This document lists value-added network (VAN) providers and B2B/B2C conduit partners identified from publicly available information. Drakes operates both **wholesale-supplied** stores (e.g. via Metcash in Queensland) and **direct-supply** operations (e.g. South Australia), with different B2B partners in each context.

---

## Business context

- **Family-owned;** 5,500+ employees; over $1 billion annual turnover.
- **Emphasis** on direct supplier relationships to reduce costs and support local manufacturers.
- **Annual Supplier Conference** — brings together executives, suppliers, and families; awards for supplier performance (e.g. meat, produce, dairy, frozen, beverages, general merchandise).

---

## Wholesale / B2B conduit — Metcash

| Partner | Role | Notes |
|---------|------|--------|
| **Metcash** | Wholesale supply to Drakes-branded stores (Queensland) | Extended supply agreement covering all **22 Drakes-branded supermarkets in Queensland** through approximately **June 2034**. Reported ~$222.5m in sales (FY24). Grocery supply; major B2B conduit for Drakes in QLD. |
| **SPS Commerce (for Metcash)** | EDI for Metcash suppliers | Suppliers to Metcash (and thus to Drakes QLD where supplied via Metcash) can use SPS Commerce for EDI: purchase orders, advance ship notices, invoices, PO acknowledgments; POS data; faster payment via PO-to-invoice matching. |

**References:** Metcash supply agreement with Drakes (e.g. AFR, Retail World); SPS Commerce — Metcash Limited: [spscommerce.com/au/network/find-a-partner/view/metcash-limited](https://www.spscommerce.com/au/network/find-a-partner/view/metcash-limited)

## Direct supply (South Australia) — EDI and VAN

Drakes moved from wholesaler supply to **direct supply** for its **South Australian stores** (e.g. 40+ stores), purchasing 26,000+ items directly from suppliers. As part of this, Drakes implemented an **EDI supplier portal** for the first time.

| Partner | Role | Notes |
|---------|------|--------|
| **SPS Commerce** | EDI / supplier portal for direct vendors | Full-service EDI solution for vendor onboarding and compliance. **40 vendors onboarded within ~9 months** (exceeded initial expectations). Improved order visibility, inventory management, and reduced receiving bottlenecks. |

**EDI document types (ANSI X12):**

- Purchase Order (850)  
- Advance Ship Notice (856)  
- Purchase Order Acknowledgment (855)  
- Invoice (810)  

**References:** SPS Commerce — Drakes case study: [spscommerce.com/resources/drakes-case-study](https://spscommerce.com/resources/drakes-case-study)
