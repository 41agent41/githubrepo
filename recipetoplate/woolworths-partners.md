## Value-added network (VAN) / EDI solution providers

Woolworths has appointed **four strategic partners** for B2B integration with its supplier community. Only one is clearly named in public sources; the full set is published on PartnerHub.

| Provider | Role | Notes |
|----------|------|--------|
| **SPS Commerce** | Strategic EDI/VAN partner | One of four strategic B2B integration partners; chosen after evaluation and certification. Uses **AS2** for B2B connectivity. Supports 100+ Woolworths suppliers and 100,000+ business documents per month. Full-service EDI: purchase orders, invoices, advance ship notices, PO acknowledgments, PO changes; POS data access; product listing support. |
| *Other strategic VAN partners* | B2B integration | Three additional strategic partners are listed on PartnerHub (Woolworths Value-Added Network Providers). Check PartnerHub for current names and scope. |

## EDI document types and standards

- **Protocol:** AS2-based B2B integration platform for real-time supplier integration.
- **Common EDI documents (ANSI X12):**
  - Purchase Order (850)
  - Purchase Order Change (860)
  - Invoice (810)
  - Purchase Order Acknowledgment (855)
  - Advance Ship Notice (856)

## BIG W (Woolworths Group)

BIG W uses the same PartnerHub ecosystem and has its own **Value-Added Network Providers** list on PartnerHub.

- **EDI standard:** EANCOM97 (subset of UN/EDIFACT, Version D96A).
- **Alternative to EDI:** Free web-based “webforms” for trade partners not yet EDI-capable (no VAN required).

**PartnerHub article:** [BIG W Value-Added Network Providers](https://partnerhub.woolworthsgroup.com.au/s/article/BIG-W-Value-Added-Network-Providers)
